03 // how it works

Three moves.
That's the whole product.

01

Meet Onyx. Two questions.

First time you open Onyx, he introduces himself and asks you what you're working on. That's onboarding. No 14 screens, no sign-up wizard.

02

Tell him what matters. Then forget him.

Link your bank. Let him see your calendar. Tell him your goals. After that, he lives in the background — learning your rhythm, watching the signals, remembering what you said.

03

He reaches out. You reply sometimes.

Morning brief. Live nudge when it matters. Night close-out. Clear, calm, a little too honest. You don't open the app. The app opens to you.

06 // questions

When does Onyx launch?

Private beta in 30 days. Waitlist order = invite order.

How much will it cost?

Free during private beta. ~$8/mo at launch. First 100 signups lock in $4/mo for life. See full pricing →

What data does Onyx see?

Only what you choose to connect: your bank, your calendar, the goals you tell him. That's it. No ads, ever. Your data is never sold. Your stuff belongs to you — export or delete it any time.

Is Onyx actually an AI?

Yes. Onyx uses a large language model to read your signals and write back in plain English. Your prompts + context go to the model we use; we never train on your data, and we don't sell it. If that's a dealbreaker, we'll tell you exactly what's sent before you connect anything.

Who is building this?

Jaime. Solo. $500 and thirty days. You can read the whole story above.
