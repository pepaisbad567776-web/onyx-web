03 // how it works

Three moves.
That's the whole product.

01

Open Onyx. Meet Onyx.

First time you open the app, Onyx introduces itself and asks you two questions. That's onboarding. No 14 screens. No sign-up wizard.

02

Log what matters. Or don't.

Money, habits, goals, whatever. Quick taps. Onyx fills in the rest and starts learning your rhythm. The more you feed it, the sharper it gets.

03

Wake up. Get read.

Every morning Onyx tells you the truth about yesterday — clear, calm, a little too honest. Your score moves. You level. You keep going.
