What we do with your data.

Short version: we collect what you give us (your email, whatever you choose to share), we use it to email you about Onyx, and we don't sell it. That's it.

What we collect

Right now, Onyx is a waitlist. The only data we collect from this site is what you voluntarily submit:

  • Your email address (when you reserve a spot or send feedback).
  • Any messages you send via the feedback form.
  • Your referral code, if you arrived via a friend's share link.
  • Basic access logs our hosting provider keeps (IP, user agent) — standard for any website.

How we use it

  • To email you when Onyx is ready for beta, and occasional updates on how the build is going.
  • To reply to your feedback if you ask us to.
  • To decide invite order (waitlist order + referrals).

Who we share it with

We use Formspree to receive waitlist and feedback submissions. Your email and messages are processed by them on our behalf. We do not share your data with any ad networks, data brokers, or third parties for marketing purposes.

When the Onyx app ships, the privacy policy will grow to cover the data you connect (bank, calendar, etc.). We'll tell you exactly what's sent, to whom, before you connect anything. Nothing happens without your explicit consent.

Your rights

  • Email hello@getonyx.app to delete your waitlist entry or any feedback you sent.
  • Ask for a copy of what we have on you at any time.
  • Opt out of emails with one click — every email we send includes a clear unsubscribe link.

Cookies

The site uses localStorage to remember your signup status and keep the waitlist counter consistent in your browser. We do not use any advertising or tracking cookies.

Changes

If we materially change this policy, we'll email anyone on the waitlist before the change takes effect.

Contact

Real human at the other end: hello@getonyx.app. J-Works HQ. Built by Jaime.
